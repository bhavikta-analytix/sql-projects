## Sales Performance Analysis

### Problem Statement
The company wants to understand sales trends, top-performing products,
and revenue contribution across regions.

### Dataset
Sales transaction data containing order date, product, region,
quantity, and revenue.

### Tools Used
- SQL
- Excel

### Analysis Performed
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top 5 products by revenue
- Region-wise revenue distribution

### Key Insights
- Top 3 products contribute ~35% of total revenue
- Q4 shows the highest sales growth
- West region generates maximum revenue

### Business Recommendation
Focus marketing and inventory planning on top-performing products,
especially during Q4, to maximize revenue.
