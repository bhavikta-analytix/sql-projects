## Sales Performance Analysis

### Problem Statement
The company wants to understand sales trends, top-performing products,
and revenue contribution across regions.

### Dataset
Sales transaction data containing order date, product, region,
quantity, and revenue.

### Tools Used
- SQL
- Excel

### Analysis Performed
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top 5 products by revenue
- Region-wise revenue distribution

### Key Insights
- The top 3 products contribute approximately 35% of total revenue, indicating strong product concentration.
- Sales peak during Q4, likely due to festive season demand and promotional campaigns.
- The West region generates the highest revenue, suggesting strong regional market performance.

### Business Recommendation
Focus marketing and inventory planning on top-performing products,
especially during Q4, to maximize revenue.

### What I Learned
- Writing aggregation queries using GROUP BY and ORDER BY
- Analyzing monthly trends using date functions
- Identifying top-performing products using revenue analysis
- Translating SQL outputs into business recommendations
